ABOUT US

The Lonely Turtle Club is a collection of 3,333 Lonely Turtles created to build a strong community in Web3. The collection was founded with the aim to provide a fun, accessible gateway to a DAO.

Our goal is to offer opportunities and utilities to the average investor. We are a community first project committed to community involvement and bringing value to every member of the Lonely Turtle Club.

ROADMAP - PHASE 1


Lonely Turtle Club Mint

    A collection of 3333 Lonely Turtles will be released.

Verify Holders Role

Holders will be able to verify their Lonely Turtles and gain access to holders perks within the Discord server.

Mint Proceeds

50% of the Mint proceeds and 100% of royalties will be sent to a treasury fund. The rest will be used to pay key team members and fund development.

Staking

Members of the Lonely Turtle Club will be able to stake their NFTS to recieve $TUR tokens.

Staking Utility

Users who have accumulated a pre determined amount of $TUR tokens will gain access to the DAO.

DAO

Holders who have gained access to the DAO will conduct vote on how to use the treasury funds.
